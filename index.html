Immerse Yourself in Swiss Sights
Swiss Alps

The Swiss Alps are one of Europe's most spectacular sights. Take a trip on the Glacier Express and take in the view as you dine and wine, or experience the silky smooth snow of the slopes with a pair of skis or a snowboard. No matter what you choose, the Swiss Alps will leave you with a sense of awe and memories forever.

Jet d'Eau

The beautiful Jet d'Eau stands tall on the majestic Geneva Lake. The aquatic beauty can rise to 140 meters, showcasing itself to anyone lucky enough to visit Geneva. Those who want a closer look at the awe-inspiring landmark can walk a pathway leading straight to the fountain.

Festivals

Switzerland is home to many fun-filled festivals. The one pictured is the International Hot Air Balloon festival, where numerous countries gather with their own balloons. Basler Fasnacht is another immersive event to see, where confetti falls like a snowstorm and locals roam around in costumes and masks.
